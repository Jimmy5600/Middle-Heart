Our Work

As associates of the American Heart Association (AHA), our team has been deeply committed to promoting awareness about heart health and the importance of life-saving skills within our communities. Over the course of our involvement, we have recognized the critical role that education and accessible resources play in encouraging people of all ages to prioritize their cardiovascular well-being. One of our primary initiatives has been the development of an informative and user-friendly website designed to serve as a central hub for heart health awareness. This platform is intended to provide valuable information on a variety of topics, including understanding heart disease, recognizing early warning signs of cardiac arrest, and learning about preventative measures that can significantly reduce the risk of heart-related conditions. We have carefully curated articles, infographics, and interactive tools to ensure that users can easily navigate and engage with the content, making heart health education both approachable and effective for a broad audience. In addition to our work on the website, we are currently in the process of producing an educational video specifically targeted towards middle school students. We firmly believe that it is never too early to start learning essential, life-saving skills, and our goal is to introduce younger audiences to the fundamental steps of performing CPR in a clear, age-appropriate, and engaging way. This video aims not only to teach the technical aspects of CPR but also to instill a sense of confidence and responsibility in students, empowering them to act decisively in emergency situations. By combining visual demonstrations with simple, easy-to-follow instructions, we hope to make this resource both memorable and impactful. Through these efforts, we are striving to contribute to the AHA’s broader mission of improving heart health across the nation and ultimately saving lives. We believe that by fostering early awareness and providing accessible tools for education, we can help build a generation that is better equipped to care for their own hearts and to assist others in moments of critical need. Our team remains dedicated to expanding these projects, reaching new audiences, and continuing to advocate for a healthier, more informed community.
