American Heart Association

The American Heart Association was founded in 1924, and its goal is to spread awareness for things such as general heart health and CPR. They want to change the future of health in the United States. According to them, deaths from cardiovascular diseases have halved since their founding, and they will continue to keep saving lives in the future.
