AHA

The American Heart Association (AHA) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving heart health and reducing deaths caused by cardiovascular diseases and stroke. Founded in 1924, the AHA has spent over a century working to raise awareness, fund research, and educate the public about the importance of maintaining a healthy heart. Its mission is to be a relentless force for a world of longer, healthier lives. Since its founding, the AHA has made significant strides in advancing medical research and promoting life-saving practices. According to the organization, deaths from cardiovascular diseases have been cut in half since 1924, thanks to improvements in early detection, treatment, and public education. The AHA also plays a crucial role in setting health guidelines and providing training in CPR and emergency cardiovascular care to both professionals and everyday people. Today, the American Heart Association continues its work by advocating for public health policies, funding critical research, and encouraging healthier lifestyles. Their message remains clear: through education, prevention, and timely treatment, countless lives can be saved. The organization remains committed to building a healthier future for all communities, leaving a lasting impact on generations to come.

JReid

JReid InDeed is a community-focused organization dedicated to making a positive difference in the lives of others. Since its founding, the organization has worked tirelessly to uplift, inspire, and support individuals through various charitable programs and outreach efforts. Their mission is rooted in compassion, unity, and service, striving to bring meaningful change to communities in need. The organization was created in honor of its namesake, with the goal of continuing a legacy of kindness, leadership, and generosity. Through events, volunteer initiatives, and partnerships, JReid InDeed aims to provide resources, opportunities, and hope to those who need it most. From back-to-school drives and food distributions to mentorship programs and health awareness campaigns, their work touches people of all ages and backgrounds. JReid InDeed believes in the power of community and the importance of standing together. By fostering connections and encouraging people to give back, the organization hopes to build a brighter, stronger future for everyone. Their ongoing commitment to service reminds others that even small actions can lead to significant, lasting change.
