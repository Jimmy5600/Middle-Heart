AHA

The American Heart Association (AHA) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to fighting heart disease and stroke. It funds cardiovascular medical research, educates the public on healthy living, and advocates for stronger public health policies. The AHA also sets standards for CPR and emergency cardiovascular care, helping to improve health outcomes nationwide.
