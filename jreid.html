JReid InDeed

JReid InDeed is a nonprofit organization founded by NFL safety Justin Reid, dedicated to creating positive change in communities through educational support, youth development programs, and health awareness initiatives. Established to give back to the communities that shaped him, Reid created the foundation to focus on providing resources and opportunities to underserved youth, with a special emphasis on education, mentorship, and wellness.
The mission of JReid InDeed is to empower young people to achieve their fullest potential both on and off the field. The organization organizes events like football camps, scholarship programs, and leadership workshops designed to teach life skills, build confidence, and offer mentorship from role models in sports and beyond. Through these programs, JReid InDeed aims to inspire youth to pursue their goals, develop strong character, and become leaders in their communities.
In addition to youth development, JReid InDeed is committed to raising awareness about important health issues and promoting overall community well-being. The foundation partners with local organizations and schools to host health screenings, educational seminars, and outreach programs that encourage healthy lifestyles and preventive care. Through its ongoing initiatives, JReid InDeed continues to make a meaningful impact in the lives of young people and families, fostering stronger, healthier, and more connected communities for the future.
